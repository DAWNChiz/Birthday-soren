April 16th, 2025 · Written with love, across the miles
My dearest Soren,

I don't know exactly how the universe works, but I believe it has a sense of humour — because it chose to bring us together over a chess game. Of all the ways two people could find each other, it had to be 64 squares and a handful of pieces. And somehow, that feels just right for us.

I want you to know something today, on your birthday — I will always be by your side. The miles between us don't change that. The time zones don't change that. Nothing will ever change that. तुम जहाँ भी हो, मैं तुम्हारे साथ हूँ।
(Wherever you are, I am with you.)

I can't hold your hand in person yet,
but I held it through a movie screen at night —
when you said, "Hey, can you hold my hand?"
— and I would have crossed oceans right then
just to be there.

Wherever you are is where I want to be.
Wherever we go, we'll go together.
Paris. Tokyo. The quiet places in between.
Every tulip garden, every sunrise, yours and mine.

You are the most caring, creative, beautiful soul I have ever known. The way you draw — you put your whole heart into things. The way you talk to me all day — you make every ordinary moment feel precious. The way you wrapped yourself in that shawl and still managed to look stunning — I think about that more than I should.

On this birthday, I promise you this — I am not going anywhere. I will stick with you through everything. Through the scary movies. Through the hard days. Through all the miles and all the waiting. तुम मेरी हो, और मैं तुम्हारा।
(You are mine, and I am yours.)

Happy Birthday, my Soren. 🌷
Thank you for existing. Thank you for being exactly who you are. The world is softer and brighter because you are in it.

Always yours, Chris 💕 ♟️ → 💌 → 🌏 — Our story is just beginning.
